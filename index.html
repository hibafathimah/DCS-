TOP COURSES

M.Tech in CSE- Specialization in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

Intended to develop Human Resources for System Integration, Data Base Design & Administration, Information Technology Enabled Services and more. A cross migratory programme where any engg. graduate can enrol, with limited entry.

M.Tech in CSE- Artificial Intelligence & Software Engineering

Intended to train Computer Engineers to become Software Engineers who can manage large software projects. They are expected to lead software design and development teams, with good proficiency in modelling, designing, testing and decision making.

M.Tech in CSE- Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

Expected to lead software design and development teams, with good proficiency in modelling, designing, testing and decision making. Computational as well as managerial skills are given adequate importance in the course content.

M.Sc in CS- Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Provides rigorous training in data science, machine learning, and data modelling with intensive industry-based exposure. Aims to equip students with multi-disciplinary skills across computer science, machine learning, statistics, and logic.
